PhD Computer Science Research Student

I'm John Charlton, a first year research student at the University of Sheffield, in the Department of Computer Science, in the Visual Computing Group.

My focus is on high performance multi agent visualisation and interactive rendering. Such examples I examine are pedestrian collision models which run in real-time for many millions of agents
