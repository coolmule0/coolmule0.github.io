PhD Computer Science Research Student

I'm John Charlton, a first year research student at the University of Sheffield, in the Department of Computer Science, in the Visual Computing Group.

My focus is on high performance pedestrian collision models. This is achieved through the expoitation of Graphical processing units (GPUs), which requires modifying current models to exploit data and compute parellalism.
